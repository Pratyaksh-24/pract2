# Hero Video Brief

## Objective
A 15-second looping video for the hero landing page that captures the essence of the Noir & Cream brand. The loop should be seamless and barely perceptible.

## Visuals
- Extreme close-up of heavy cream being poured in slow motion into a glass of Kahlúa and ice.
- Dark, cinematic lighting with a radial-gradient overlay from transparent center to dark edges.
- High contrast, emphasizing the texture of the cream and the deep amber of the liqueur.

## Technical Specs
- Format: MP4 / WebM (for web optimization)
- Length: 8-15 seconds
- Framerate: 60fps (for smooth slow motion)
- Audio: None (muted by default)

# Photography Directions Brief

## Style & Mood
- Dark studio photography — single dramatic light source, high contrast.
- Slow, sensual, midnight. The moment before the first sip.

## Key Shots

### Hero Shot
- Close-up overhead pour — cream cascading into dark coffee liqueur over ice, captured mid-swirl.

### Product Shots
1. **90° Side Glass**: Deep bokeh black bg, condensation droplets lit rim-light.
2. **Overhead Flat-Lay**: Glass on obsidian marble, Kahlúa bottle, cream jug, coffee beans.
3. **Hands Cradling the Glass**: Warmth meets cold, focus on knuckles and rim.

## Avoid
- Bright white backgrounds
- Stock photo clichés
- Over-saturated colors
